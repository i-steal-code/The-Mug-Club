How readiness is computed

  1. Each prep row points at a reusable component (e.g. Matcha cloud, Strawberry flavour).
  2. The component lists its inventory ingredients and the grams used to produce one base yield.
  3. required = qty_to_prep × (qty_g_per_yield ÷ base_yield) for every ingredient line.
  4. If every ingredient has enough stock, the row is Ready; otherwise it's Short with the gap shown.
  5. Rows without a linked component or an ingredient list show as Unmapped until a component recipe is filled in.
